Getting started

This tool lets you describe each Weatherbird image out loud and automatically extracts tags for the picker. No AI — just your voice.

  1. Select all images from your weatherbirds folder below
  2. Click the mic button and describe what you see (ignoring the bird)
  3. Edit or remove tags as needed
  4. Click Save & Next to move on
  5. When done, download and upload weatherbird-index.json to GitHub
